Metro Boomin: Hip-Hop's Living Legend

Dive into the world of Metro Boomin, the beat magician who's been setting the hip-hop scene on fire. Born on September 16, 1993, in St. Louis, Missouri, Metro's been cooking up beats since day one. You've probably heard his signature tagline, "If Young Metro don't trust you, I'm gon' shoot you," echoing through your favorite tracks. From Future to Drake, Metro's the guy behind the beats that make you nod your head without even realizing it. Get ready to vibe with the beats and stories that make Metro Boomin a legend in the game.

A Look Into Metro's Life

Musical Upbringing


Growing up in a family with a strong musical background, Metro Boomin was exposed to a diverse range of genres, laying the foundation for his future in music.

Introduction to Beat Making


In his early years, Metro began experimenting with beat-making software, showcasing an early aptitude for crafting unique sounds and rhythms.

Recognition in High School


Even during his teenage years, Metro's talent didn't go unnoticed. His beats gained popularity among peers, setting the stage for his entrance into the professional music scene.

Connection with OJ Da Juiceman


During high school, Metro Boomin's connection with rapper OJ Da Juiceman marked a significant milestone. This collaboration provided Metro with valuable experience and exposure in the hip-hop industry.

Move to Atlanta


In pursuit of a vibrant music scene, Metro Boomin relocated to Atlanta during his teenage years, a move that would prove pivotal in his career.

Early Industry Collaborations


By his late teenage years, Metro had already collaborated with prominent artists such as Gucci Mane, gaining recognition as a formidable force in hip-hop production. These early collaborations set the stage for his future success
