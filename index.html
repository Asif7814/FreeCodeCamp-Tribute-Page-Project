Metro Boomin: Crafting Beats, Shaping Hits, Living Legend

Dive into the world of Metro Boomin, the beat magician who's been setting the hip-hop scene on fire. Born on September 16, 1993, in St. Louis, Missouri, Metro's been cooking up beats since day one. You've probably heard his signature tagline, "If Young Metro don't trust you, I'm gon' shoot you," echoing through your favorite tracks. From Future to Drake, Metro's the guy behind the beats that make you nod your head without even realizing it. Get ready to vibe with the beats and stories that make Metro Boomin a legend in the game.
